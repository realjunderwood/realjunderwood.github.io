Legg-Walcut Cemetery

The Legg-Walcut Cemetery is a historic cemetery located on a small plot near the Wellington School in Upper Arlington, Ohio.

Bickett-Richards Cemetery

Convienently located behind a Safeway gas station, the Bickett-Richards cemetery was restored by volunteers in 2003 following years of neglect.

Union Cemetery

Established in 1847, Union Cemetery is the resting place of more than 66,000 individuals. Pleasant Litchford, a former enslaved person who settled much of the land that is now Upper Arlington, is buried here.
